About

A multi-dimensional artist, a pioneer of sound within the new age of music, technology, and culture. A native of Los Angeles,who knows the music, the spots, the business, and the people, Speiser(DJ) is a complete audiophile with a goal of creating a larger and closer musical community.

Quick on the decks, he is always ready to play. He has performed for just about every kind of event and is always ready for more. He sorts his music daily and with music evolving by the day he has compiled the greatest ever-growing playlist called "TOP5electric" (TRILLION DOLLAR PLAYLIST). Who's ready to party?

Speiser (DJ) is not about the individual DJ. It's about the collective individuality we achieve through expression.
